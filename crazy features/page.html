+++ The Wormhole Drive +++
Kinda like a stargate from the Stargate:SG1 show, where a wormhole opens up to an intended target system be it either a W-space system or a designated K-space system.  We could hijack a current module like the jump drive and make new entries in the DB for it, then use force field password to "set" the target system by name or locus signature.  When activated, the wormhole entry point is created, but the wormhole drive-carrying ship cannot move until the module is disengaged and the current cycle completes, but the wormhole entry point stays active until then allowing ships to come out and go in.  When module deactivates, the wormhole drive-carrying ship has 30sec to enter wormhole before despawn.  To make this item of strategic and tactical importance and risk balanced, make the ship have to run out a full cycle once before the wormhole opens up, so at least the ship and others are vulnerable while waiting for the first cycle to complete.  Make the cycle time kinda long, say 1 minute or maybe 5, and use some advanced science skill to reduce the cycle time by 10% per level.

+++ Ship Explosions Do Damage to Nearby Ships Regardless of Friend/ Foe +++
This would be very realistic to make ship explosions dispense damage to nearby ships, proportional to how close they are at the time of the explosion with the damage amount dropping off with distance according to the inverse square law.  Ship capacitor amount would translate into half Thermal half EM damage along with any loaded cap booster charges and charges in cargo "selected" for destruction, ship mass would translate into kinetic damage, and loaded missiles/bombs and any missiles/bombs/jump or POS  fuel in cargo "selected" for destruction rather than loot drop would translate into explosive damage.


+++ Titan SuperWeapon Area-of-Effect Damage +++
The Titan SuperWeapons are targeted modules now, however, we want to make them do damage to nearby ships as well as the primary target.  They do 2 million hp of damage the majority of which should be applied to the primary target, however, a good portion should be distributed to nearby ships with damage amounts applied using the radio frequency transmission energy falloff equation (inverse square law) as a function of distance away from the transmission point, such that ships very close by the primary target get hit badly, and ships at some distance away receive some damage and ships far away receive little to no damage.  So, just to be clear, if more than one ship is in effective radius of the weapon, more than 2 million hp will most likely be dispensed since the primary target will receive random hp between 80% and 100% of the 2 million hp and that destruction wave propagates outward in all directions as a sphere, with an amount of damage dispensed to each ship between 80% and 100% of the magnitude of 2 million hp times the inverse square law's resulting fraction of damage at radius 0 as a function of the distance of each ships from radius 0, the initial impact point of the weapon.  Titan SuperWeapon skill level should boost effective radius from a base by 5-10% per level.  Perhaps attach a new attribute to the superweapon modules for base damage radius and attach new attribute to superweapon skills of percent increase in that radius per level.

When warping to a planet/moon/star, set destination coordinates to ther point on the sphere at altitude that faces the current ship position

       1. determine if desired destination point is 'behind' the planet such that the ship's warp vector from the point of origin would take it through the perpendicular circle formed by the plane/moon/star center point and its radius plus some buffer.  if not, allow warp directly to desired destination point, otherwise, compute the final position on the orbit sphere
           - determine if vector will take ship through planet if TBD
           - distance traveled will equal TBD
